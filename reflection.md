# Reflection Notes: Profile Comparisons

## Pairwise Output Comparisons

- High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi:
  - Pop profile pushed energetic, danceable songs like Sunrise City and Gym Hero because the genre/mood and energy targets were high.
  - Chill Lofi profile shifted strongly toward Library Rain and Midnight Coding because of lofi+chill matching and higher acoustic preference.

- High-Energy Pop vs Deep Intense Rock:
  - Both profiles liked high-energy tracks, but rock+intense profile ranked Storm Runner first due to direct genre/mood match.
  - Pop profile favored brighter valence songs; rock profile accepted lower valence tracks with similar intensity.

- Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock:
  - Chill Lofi rewarded lower tempo, calmer mood, and acousticness.
  - Deep Intense Rock rewarded high energy and non-acoustic traits, producing louder and faster recommendations.

## Why This Makes Sense

The profile settings are effectively testing different vibe definitions. When energy, mood, and acoustic preference move together, output changes in intuitive directions. This confirms the recommender is using the intended features rather than ranking randomly.
